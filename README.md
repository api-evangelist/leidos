# Leidos (leidos)
Leidos, a Fortune 500 digital and mission innovator, delivers solutions at the intersection of national security, health, and critical infrastructure. With a global workforce of approximately 47,000, the company is executing its NorthStar 2030 strategy focused on five growth engines: space and maritime, energy infrastructure, digital modernization and cyber, mission software, and managed health.
